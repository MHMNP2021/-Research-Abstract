# -Research-Abstract
Unpublished Social Research Abstract about E-Banking Satisfaction among Undergraduates at the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka.

This is social research to get an idea about E-Banking, E-Banking Services, E-Customer Loyalty, and E-Customer Satisfaction. The credit for this abstract goes to M.H.M.N. Perera and N.A.A. Thisaranee (UG of Financial Mathematics and Industrial Statistics, University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka). The research was done partially to fulfill the degree program.
